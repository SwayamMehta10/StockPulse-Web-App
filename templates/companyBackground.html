ISS Governance Quality Score

The Governance QualityScore is a data-driven tool that helps investors assess corporate governance risk in their portfolios. It's a dynamic, relative governance profile scoring solution used by leading institutional investors. The score is based on publicly disclosed data on a company's governance practices and covers four key areas of corporate governance: Board structure, Compensation, Shareholder rights, and Audit and risk oversight. The score looks at 220 individual factors, including qualitative aspects of governance, such as global governance standards and alignment with ESG voting policy according to region.
